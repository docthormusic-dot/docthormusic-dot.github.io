4. Embedded SoundCloud player (consent-based)

The Music section can display a player from SoundCloud (SoundCloud Global Limited & Co. KG, Rheinsberger Str. 76/77, 10115 Berlin, Germany). The player does not load automatically. No connection to SoundCloud is established, no SoundCloud resource is requested, and no data is transmitted to SoundCloud until you actively press the "Load SoundCloud player" button.

If you press the button, SoundCloud receives technical data such as your IP address and may store or access information on your device (e.g. cookies or similar technologies) for functional, analytics, advertising or profiling purposes, and may transfer data to countries outside the EU. The legal bases are your consent pursuant to § 25 (1) TDDDG for the storage of or access to information on your device, and Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR for the associated processing of personal data.

You can withdraw your consent at any time via the "Withdraw SoundCloud consent" control in the Music section (or by deleting this website's data in your browser); withdrawal takes effect for the future, and the page reloads without any SoundCloud content. The website remains fully usable without loading the player — all music platforms are also available via plain links. Details: SoundCloud Privacy Policy.

5. Storage of your consent choice

If you press "Load SoundCloud player", we store the value granted under the key docthor-soundcloud-consent in your browser's localStorage. This entry exists solely to remember your choice so you are not asked again on every visit; it contains no identifier and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. It remains until you withdraw consent or delete the site's browser data. Storing this entry is necessary to honour your expressed choice (§ 25 (2) TDDDG; Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR).

6. External links

Links to Spotify, Beatport, Apple Music, SoundCloud, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and Dropbox (press kit) are plain links. No data is transferred to these providers until you click them; from then on, the respective provider's privacy policy applies.

7. Contact by email

If you contact us by email (e.g. booking requests), we process the data you provide (email address, message content, technical email metadata) to handle your request (Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR where the request relates to a booking or other contract, otherwise Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR). Our mailbox is operated by Google's Gmail service; Google therefore processes messages sent to this address, including their metadata, as our email provider under its own terms and privacy policy. We keep correspondence for as long as needed to handle the request and any statutory retention periods. Providing your data is not legally required, but we cannot answer requests without contact details.

8. No analytics, no profiling by us

We do not use analytics or advertising services and do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling.
