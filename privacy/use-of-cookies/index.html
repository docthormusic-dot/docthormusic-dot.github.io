Use of Cookies

Cookies set by this website

None. This website does not set any cookies of its own, does not use local storage for tracking, and does not use any analytics or advertising technology. That is why you do not see a cookie consent banner here — there is nothing to consent to on our side.

Third-party content that may set cookies

The Music section embeds the official SoundCloud player. When the player loads (as you scroll near the Music section), SoundCloud may set cookies or use similar technologies inside the player frame — for example to remember playback preferences or for its own analytics. These cookies are controlled by SoundCloud, not by us. Details: SoundCloud Cookie Policy.

How to control cookies

  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time (usually under Privacy & Security).
  • Most browsers can block third-party cookies specifically — this stops embedded players from setting cookies while the rest of this site keeps working fully.
  • If you block the SoundCloud player entirely, you can still listen via the direct platform links in the Music section.
