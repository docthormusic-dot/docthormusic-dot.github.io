Use of Cookies

Cookies set by this website

None. This website does not set any cookies of its own and does not use any analytics or advertising technology.

Storage this website uses

The site uses exactly one browser-storage entry: if you press "Load SoundCloud player" in the Music section, the value granted is stored under the key docthor-soundcloud-consent in your browser's localStorage. Purpose: remembering your consent choice so you are not asked again. Duration: until you withdraw consent or delete this site's browser data. This entry is strictly necessary to honour your choice, contains no identifier, and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-party storage (SoundCloud, only after consent)

The Music section can embed the official SoundCloud player. It does not load automatically: no connection to SoundCloud is made until you press "Load SoundCloud player". After you consent, SoundCloud may set cookies or use similar technologies inside the player frame — for example for playback preferences, analytics, advertising or profiling. These are controlled by SoundCloud, not by us. Details: SoundCloud Cookie Policy.

Giving and withdrawing consent

  • Giving consent: only by pressing the "Load SoundCloud player" button. Scrolling, continued browsing or any other interaction is never treated as consent.
  • Withdrawing consent: press "Withdraw SoundCloud consent" beneath the player (the page reloads without SoundCloud content), or delete this site's data in your browser settings.
  • Without consent, the whole site remains fully usable — the music platforms are also reachable via plain links.
  • Independently of this, you can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time (usually under Privacy & Security).
