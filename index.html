// About

DOCTHOR is a rising force in hard techno — relentless, high-intensity sets and productions built on raw energy and unique soundscapes.

Emerging in mid-2022, the German producer and DJ channels the iconic Schranz era of the 2000s and 2010s into a signature sound of his own: dark, distorted and driving. Born in Stuttgart and now moving between Valencia and Stuttgart, he played his first gig at Haus33 in Nuremberg — and hasn't slowed down since.
